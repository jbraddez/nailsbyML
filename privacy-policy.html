Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 August 2025

BYML ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information on byml.co.uk.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Information you provide voluntarily via our contact form, including your name, email address, and message.
  • Usage data collected through Google Analytics, such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and browser type.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • Analyse website traffic and improve our website using Google Analytics data.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Information submitted through the contact form is sent directly to the website owner.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for advertising. Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data to help us improve the site. Users cannot opt out of Google Analytics tracking via this website.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures such as HTTPS to protect the information submitted through the contact form and collected via Google Analytics. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about the personal data you submit via the contact form. While there is no opt-out for Google Analytics tracking on this website, your rights regarding the information you provide directly are protected under UK data protection laws.
